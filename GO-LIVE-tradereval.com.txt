In SQUARESPACE (your Google domain):
1. Log in at https://account.squarespace.com
2. Domains → tradereval.com → DNS Settings
3. Add the records Vercel shows you, usually:

   Type    Host    Value
   ----    ----    -----

   CNAME   www     cname.vercel-dns.com

4. Save. Wait 5 minutes to 48 hours (often under 1 hour)
5. Vercel will show green checkmark when connected
